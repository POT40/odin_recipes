Bro... this dish have all u need: brain, spinal cord, the strongest bones (most calcium). And you will be surprised, but it is so tasty... It worth it 100%

Ingredients

  • human - 1 peace
  • vinegear - 1 l
  • sugar - 500 g

Steps

  1. cut head off the cold body (You can use the same body from the previous recipe)
  2. put the head to the jar
  3. put the vinegear
  4. put the sugar
  5. mix it :)
  6. put the jar in the refrigerator or any other cold place (for example, a basement) for one week
  7. Enjoy the meal :)
