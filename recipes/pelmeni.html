Pelmeni

What is pelmeni?

Pelmeni is the best russian dish, it's including all the fuck your body needs: meat - protein, dough - carbohydrates. So, run to the nearest grocery market and buy all the stuff u will need.

Ingredients

  • pack of pelmeni (430 g)
  • sauce (sour, mayonese, ketchup, whatever u want)
  • seasoning (1 g)

Steps

  1. boil water
  2. put pelmeni to the pot for 5 mins
  3. get pelmeni to the pen on the weak fire
  4. bring oil and mix the pelmeni
  5. fry pelmeni for 10 mins mixing them
  6. add seasoning
  7. put pelmeni to the bowl and add your sauce
  8. the best dish in the world is ready!
